How To Use This App?

  • Step 1: Click on the blue '+' button at the top to enter a reminder.
  • Step 2: Enter the name of your reminder.
  • Step 3: You will be able to check, delete or modify your reminder using the check, delete and modify button now.
  • Step 4: Select the reminder date and time in the date-time input box.
  • Step 5: Set your reminder by clicking on the set reminder button.
  • Step 6: Now an alarm will ring after the due date and time is over.
  • Step 7: You can snooze and stop the alarm according to your need.
